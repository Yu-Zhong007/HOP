HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

download Panthom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to Raydium.io.

Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $HOP. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Panthom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium. Go to raydium.io in google chrome or on the browser inside your Panthom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the ​$HOP token address into Raydium, select ​$HOP, and confirm. When Panthom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Swap SOL for HistoryOfPepe

Swap SOL for ​$HOP. We have Zero taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
